Furiosa: A Mad Max Saga

2024 148 min ★ 8.2/10 R
Director: George Miller Distributed by: Warner Bros. Release Date: May 24, 2024 Running Time: 2h 28m Budget: $168 million Box office: $430 million

Watch the Trailer

Plot Summary

The origin story of Imperator Furiosa, tracing her journey from a young girl taken from the Green Place of Many Mothers to becoming the fierce warrior of "Mad Max: Fury Road." Captured by the warlord Dementus and his biker horde, Furiosa must survive the brutal wasteland politics between Dementus and Immortan Joe, forging her skills and resolve in a high-octane saga of revenge and survival.

Why You Should Watch

  • Masterful Action: George Miller's signature vehicular mayhem reaches new heights with 15-minute uninterrupted chase sequences.
  • Anya Taylor-Joy's Breakout: A star-making performance that honors Charlize Theron's iconic portrayal while making the role her own.
  • World-Building: Expands the Wasteland mythology with new factions like "The Biker Horde" and "The Citadel's War Boys."
  • Technical Brilliance: Oscar-worthy practical effects and cinematography that redefine post-apocalyptic visuals.

Review

"Furiosa" is a masterclass in prequel storytelling, avoiding nostalgia traps to deliver a standalone epic. Taylor-Joy commands the screen with minimal dialogue, her expressive eyes conveying Furiosa's transformation from victim to strategist. Hemsworth steals scenes as the flamboyantly cruel Dementus, while Miller's action choreography—particularly a jaw-dropping "War Rig vs. Bike Horde" sequence—sets new benchmarks for practical stunts. Though less relentless than "Fury Road," its deeper character work and political intrigue enrich the Mad Max mythos.
