Plot Summary

In this highly anticipated sequel, Moana answers a mysterious call from her ancestors, leading her on an unexpected voyage across the Pacific with Maui and a new crew of unlikely seafarers. The journey will test her wayfinding skills like never before, revealing ancient myths and confronting supernatural threats tied to the ocean's hidden magic.

Note: Official synopsis may expand as release approaches.

  • Return of Auliʻi Cravalho and Dwayne Johnson as Moana and Maui
