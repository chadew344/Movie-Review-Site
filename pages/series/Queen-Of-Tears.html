Queen of Tears

2024 16 Episodes ★ 8.2/10 TV-14
Writer: Park Ji-eun Director: Jang Young-woo, Kim Hee-won Release Date: March 9 - April 28, 2024 Network: tvN, Netflix Genre: Romance, Drama, Comedy

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

This emotional rollercoaster follows the turbulent marriage of Hong Hae-in (Kim Ji-won), the cold heiress of Queens Group conglomerate, and Baek Hyun-woo (Kim Soo-hyun), her humble husband from a rural background. When Hae-in is diagnosed with a terminal illness and her family faces a corporate takeover, the couple must navigate love, betrayal, and redemption as they rediscover why they fell in love.

Review

"Queen of Tears" masterfully balances melodrama with unexpected humor, anchored by Kim Soo-hyun and Kim Ji-won's electric chemistry. While the terminal illness trope isn't new, the series elevates it with sharp writing and complex family dynamics. The first half delivers laugh-out-loud moments while the second half will leave viewers reaching for tissues. Some plot twists strain credibility, but the emotional payoff and stellar supporting cast (especially the hilarious Queens family) make this 2024's most addictive K-drama.
