Senna

2024 1 Season ★ 8.5/10 TV-MA
Creator: Vicente Amorim Writers: Vicente Amorim, João Emanuel Carneiro Release Date: March 21, 2024 Episodes: 6 (50m each) Network: Netflix Genre: Drama, Sports, Biography

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

This gripping limited series chronicles the life of legendary Formula 1 driver Ayrton Senna, from his early days in Brazil to his tragic death at the 1994 San Marino Grand Prix. Through archival footage and dramatic reenactments, the series explores Senna's fierce rivalry with Alain Prost, his spiritual depth, and his relentless pursuit of perfection on the track.

Why You Should Watch

  • Brings Senna's electrifying career and personal struggles to life
  • Stunning racing sequences filmed with cutting-edge technology
  • Deep dive into F1's most iconic rivalry (Senna vs. Prost)
  • Humanizes the legend beyond his public persona
  • Complements the acclaimed 2010 documentary with new perspectives

Review

"Senna" delivers a visceral, emotionally charged portrait of racing's most enigmatic champion. Leone's transformative performance captures Senna's intensity and vulnerability, while the series' hybrid format (mixing drama with real footage) creates unparalleled authenticity. Though some creative liberties are taken, the essence of Senna's legacy—his faith, patriotism, and uncompromising drive—shines through. A must-watch for motorsport fans and drama lovers alike.

Behind the Scenes

  • Senna's family granted unprecedented access to personal archives.
  • Actual F1 tracks (like Imola and Monaco) were recreated with CGI.
  • Gabriel Leone trained for 6 months to master Senna's mannerisms.
