Stranger Things

2016-Present 4 Seasons ★ 8.6/10 TV-14
Creators: The Duffer Brothers Release Date: July 15, 2016 - Present Episodes: 34 (42-139m each) Network: Netflix Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

In 1983 Hawkins, Indiana, young Will Byers vanishes mysteriously. As friends, family and local police search for answers, they are drawn into an extraordinary mystery involving top-secret government experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one strange little girl with psychokinetic abilities. What starts as a small-town disappearance soon unravels into a multi-season saga blending sci-fi, horror and 80s nostalgia, as the characters battle threats from the sinister alternate dimension known as the Upside Down.

Review

"Stranger Things" became an instant cultural phenomenon by masterfully blending Spielbergian wonder with King-like horror. The Duffer Brothers created a richly detailed 80s universe that feels both nostalgic and fresh, anchored by phenomenal performances from both child actors (especially Millie Bobby Brown) and veterans like Winona Ryder. While later seasons expanded the scope sometimes at the expense of intimacy, the show maintains its emotional core - a group of misfit kids facing cosmic horrors with friendship and courage. The perfect balance of humor, heart and horror made it Netflix's flagship original series.
