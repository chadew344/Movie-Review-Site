The Day of the Jackal

2024 1 Season ★ 8.1/10 TV-MA
Creator: Ronan Bennett Writers: Ronan Bennett, Frederick Forsyth (based on novel) Release Date: June 7, 2024 Episodes: 8 (45m each) Network: Peacock Genre: Thriller, Crime, Political Drama

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

This high-stakes thriller reimagines Frederick Forsyth's classic novel about a professional assassin known only as "The Jackal" who is hired to kill French President Charles de Gaulle in 1963. The series expands on the original story with deeper character development, showing parallel narratives of the meticulous assassin preparing his mission and the determined investigator racing to uncover his identity before it's too late.

Why You Should Watch

  • Modern update of one of the greatest political thrillers ever written
  • Breathtaking cat-and-mouse tension between hunter and hunted
  • Gorgeous 1960s European period detail
  • Expands the original story with new subplots and characters

Review

"The Day of the Jackal" masterfully updates the Cold War thriller for modern audiences while preserving the novel's meticulous tension. Ramírez delivers a chilling performance as the calculating assassin, while the series' expanded scope allows for richer political context and character development. The period production design is flawless, and the cat-and-mouse game builds to an almost unbearable suspense. A worthy successor to both the novel and the 1973 film adaptation.

Behind the Scenes
