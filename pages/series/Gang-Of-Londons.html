Plot Summary

When the head of London's most powerful crime family is assassinated, his youngest son Sean Wallace (Joe Cole) takes control and declares war on the mysterious killer. This brutal power struggle exposes the complex web of international gangs controlling London - from Albanian heroin smugglers to Nigerian drug lords and Pakistani syndicates. As alliances shift and bodies pile up, undercover cop Elliot Finch (Sope Dirisu) finds himself trapped between duty and survival.

Review

"Gangs of London" redefines crime drama with its unparalleled action sequences and sprawling narrative ambition. While the plot occasionally strains credibility, the series compensates with breathtaking violence and fascinating cultural detail about London's immigrant gang ecosystems. Sope Dirisu delivers a star-making performance as the morally conflicted Elliot, while Gareth Evans' direction (especially in Season 1) sets new standards for TV action. The show loses some momentum in Season 2 but remains one of television's most visceral viewing experiences.

Behind the Scenes
