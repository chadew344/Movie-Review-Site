Landman

2024 1 Season ★ 8.2/10 TV-MA
Creators: Taylor Sheridan, Christian Wallace Release Date: November 17, 2024 Episodes: 10 Network: Paramount+ Genre: Drama, Western

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

Landman explores the turbulent and high-stakes world of West Texas oil drilling. Centered on Tommy Norris, a veteran landman navigating the corporate and personal complications of fossil fuel fortune, the series dives deep into themes of ambition, sacrifice, and survival amid resource exploitation.

Why You Should Watch

  • Created by Taylor Sheridan of Yellowstone fame
  • Gripping performances by Billy Bob Thornton and Demi Moore
  • Authentic depiction of the oil boomtown lifestyle
  • Complex character dynamics and social themes
  • High production value and dramatic West Texas landscapes

Review

Landman delivers an atmospheric and grounded portrayal of an underexplored American industry. Thornton anchors the show with a layered, compelling performance. The writing maintains a tension between corporate ambition and working-class resilience. Sheridan's storytelling blends grit and nuance, making for a captivating drama.

Behind the Scenes

  • Inspired by the podcast Boomtown by Christian Wallace
  • Filmed in Fort Worth and other parts of Texas
  • Produced by 101 Studios and Bosque Ranch Productions
