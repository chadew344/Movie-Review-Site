Dark Matter

2024 1 Season ★ 7.7/10 TV-MA
Creator: Blake Crouch (based on his novel) Showrunner: Blake Crouch Release Date: May 8, 2024 Episodes: 9 (45-55m each) Network: Apple TV+ Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller, Mystery

Official Trailer

Plot Summary

Jason Dessen, a physicist and family man, is abducted into an alternate version of his life where he never married or had a child. As he navigates this strange parallel reality, he discovers the terrifying implications of quantum superposition and the multiverse. The series explores profound questions about identity, choice, and the roads not taken, as Jason fights to return to his true family while evading dangerous versions of himself.

Why You Should Watch

  • Mind-bending adaptation of Blake Crouch's bestselling novel
  • Complex exploration of quantum physics and alternate realities
  • Joel Edgerton's gripping dual performance
  • Visually stunning depiction of the multiverse
  • Thought-provoking themes about life choices and identity

Review

"Dark Matter" delivers a cerebral sci-fi thriller that stays remarkably faithful to its source material while expanding the story for television. Joel Edgerton shines in his dual role, capturing both versions of Jason with nuanced differences. The series balances complex quantum theory with raw emotional stakes, though some viewers may find the middle episodes slow-paced. Visually inventive with its multiverse transitions and strong supporting performances (particularly Jennifer Connelly), it's one of 2024's most intellectually satisfying sci-fi offerings.

Behind the Scenes

  • Author Blake Crouch served as showrunner and wrote every episode
  • Used practical effects for most quantum realm sequences
  • Chicago locations were meticulously recreated across different universes
  • Original novel was optioned for TV immediately after publication in 2016
